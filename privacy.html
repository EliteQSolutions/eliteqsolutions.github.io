Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to scope and deliver your Strategic Financial Intelligence Review (SFIR):

  • Your name, email address, and firm/organization name
  • Matter type, target entity details, and case description
  • Client-supplied documents (receipts, correspondence, screenshots)
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe — we never store card numbers)

2. What We Never Collect

Elite Q never requests or processes:

  • Target passwords, bank credentials, or full card numbers
  • Social Security numbers or government-issued ID numbers
  • Non-public personal data, credit reports, or identity files
  • Information about the target that is not publicly accessible

3. How We Use Your Information

All client-supplied information is used exclusively to scope, research, and deliver the contracted SFIR report. We do not use client data for marketing, resale, or any purpose beyond the engagement scope.

4. Data Processing & Security

All client-supplied documents are processed in sandboxed, encrypted environments. We employ DOD-informed operational security practices including encrypted storage, access-controlled file handling, and secure deletion protocols.

5. Data Retention & Disposal

Client-supplied records and working files are retained for a maximum of 30 calendar days after final report delivery. After this period, all client data is permanently deleted from our systems. Delivered reports (the final PDF) may be retained for quality assurance and dispute resolution purposes for up to 90 days.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — Secure payment processing
  • Formspree — Form submission handling
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting
  • Google Fonts — Typography delivery

We do not share client data with any third party beyond what is strictly necessary for payment processing and form submission.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at jaqualla@eliteqsolutions.com. We will confirm deletion within 5 business days.
