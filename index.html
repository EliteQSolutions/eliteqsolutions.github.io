The Foundation of Your Strategy

We organize the chaos of raw client documents into three distinct, actionable pillars.

I.

Timeline Reconstruction

A rigorous, chronological factual record synthesized from client documents and communications, exposing the precise sequence of events before intake.

II.

Evidence Cataloguing

We inventory client-provided records, surfacing critical gaps, missing authorizations, and correlating existing files to the established timeline.

III.

Decision-Maker Mapping

We trace institutional actors, delineating reporting structures and documented involvement to give you a clear map of organizational liability.
