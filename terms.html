1. Service Description

Elite Q Solutions LLC ("Elite Q," "we," "us") provides the Strategic Financial Intelligence Review (SFIR), a pre-litigation public-record evidence mapping service. The SFIR compiles publicly available information and client-supplied records into a structured, source-traceable PDF intelligence brief for use by family law practitioners and divorce attorneys.

2. What We Are Not

Elite Q Solutions LLC is a technology-enabled research company. We do not operate as:

  • A licensed private investigation agency
  • A law firm or provider of legal advice
  • A financial auditor or certified fraud examiner
  • A forensic laboratory or digital forensics provider
  • A fund recovery or asset recovery service

Our reviews are advisory compilations of public and client-provided records. They do not constitute legal advice, expert testimony, or definitive declarations of guilt or liability.

3. Service Terms

  • Price: $250 flat fee per review (1 target entity)
  • Turnaround: 3 business days after validated payment and intake
  • Deliverable: 4–6 page evidence-traceable PDF with a maximum of 5 material findings
  • Communication: Written-only (email). No telephone or video consultations
  • Payment: 100% upfront via Stripe. No work begins until payment clears
  • Revision: One limited revision for factual corrections within 14 days of delivery

4. Scope Boundaries

The SFIR includes:

  • Public domain registration and hosting history lookup
  • Corporate registry and business filing verification
  • Comparison of target terms of service, privacy statements, and public disclaimers
  • Chronology synthesis of client-supplied interaction logs
  • Payment-channel and public merchant descriptor mapping

The SFIR excludes:

  • Direct target contact or communication
  • Asset seizure, fund recovery, or chargeback filing
  • Non-public database access (credit reports, identity files)
  • Active intrusion, penetration testing, or malware analysis
  • Legal advice, expert testimony, or legal declarations
  • Dark-web scanning or device forensics (deferred higher-tier capability)

5. Refund Policy

Our refund policy is structured around five canonical rules:

  1. Declined engagement: Full refund if Elite Q declines the engagement before research begins.
  2. Unidentifiable subject: Full refund if the subject cannot be reliably identified through public sources before research begins.
  3. Inability to deliver: Full or proportionate refund if Elite Q cannot deliver the agreed scope due to our limitations.
  4. Completed scope: No refund after research begins when the agreed scope has been completed and delivered.
  5. Dissatisfaction limit: Client dissatisfaction alone does not create a refund obligation when the defined work was delivered as scoped.
