Frequently Asked Questions

What is this, exactly? ↓
Elite Q Solutions is a forensic intelligence and OSINT research firm. We use open-source intelligence methodology — public records, regulatory filings, digital archives, corporate databases, and web-layer sources — to compile structured, factual intelligence reports on individuals, entities, or corporate structures. We are not a law firm or a private investigation agency. Think of us as a highly specialized research analyst delivering a structured, source-cited brief built to support your pre-litigation strategy.
Is this legal advice? ↓
No. Elite Q Solutions does not provide legal advice, legal strategy, or legal representation of any kind. All deliverables are factual research compilations drawn from publicly accessible open-source intelligence. How you interpret, deploy, or apply those facts within a legal strategy is entirely within your purview as retained counsel. We provide the intelligence layer. You provide the legal expertise. There is no attorney-client relationship created between Elite Q Solutions and your client.
Who uses this service? ↓
Our primary clients in this validation phase are solo litigation attorneys and small litigation firms handling civil, commercial, or financial disputes where pre-discovery intelligence provides a material advantage. We also work with forensic accountants, licensed private investigation firms, and corporate general counsel managing pre-litigation risk. If you are bringing a case where the opposing party's financial structure, public contradictions, or digital footprint could shift case value — this is built for you.
What exactly is delivered? ↓
You receive a structured PDF intelligence brief. Depending on scope, this includes: entity and corporate network mapping, statement and filing contradiction analysis, digital footprint summary, asset and property tracing from public records, and an anomaly and risk flag section. Every finding is cited with a direct, retrievable source. No speculation. No interpretation beyond what the data shows. The report is formatted to be readable by attorneys and non-attorney stakeholders alike.
What is the turnaround time? ↓
The Validation Package is delivered within 3–5 business days from confirmed scope and payment. The Pre-Litigation Deep Audit is delivered within 7–14 business days. Rush engagements (48–72 hour delivery) are available at a premium and subject to current capacity. Turnaround is confirmed in writing at the time of engagement. Timeline integrity is non-negotiable — we do not take on more than we can deliver on time.
How is my submission handled? ↓
All submissions are treated as strictly confidential from receipt. An NDA is available upon request before any substantive case information is shared. We do not share client identities, case details, or deliverable contents with any third party. Work product is delivered to a secure channel specified by you. Our founder held a DOD Secret clearance — confidentiality is not a marketing claim here; it is an operating standard.
